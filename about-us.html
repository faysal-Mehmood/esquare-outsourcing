story

Our Story

e-square(Pvt) Ltd. was founded in Pakistan in 2006 by Javed Ahmed Sheikh(https://www.linkedin.com/in/javed-sheikh-bb112711/). From a modest beginning with few employees and HR outsourcing portfolio in couple of cities, we have grown as a trusted outsourcing partner with footprint in over 200+ locations with almost 2 decades of consistent accolade & recognition from diversified clientele. Initially, we started as a small team of 8 individuals providing basic HR outsourcing services to local businesses. We have developed and broadened our services globally throughout this time to meet the needs of several industries, including healthcare, Telecommunication, Finance, ,,Pharmaceutical, Medicine, Engineering, B2B, Modern Trade, Distribution, ecommerce, logistics, courier, IT, Healthcare, and more.(Industries to be Added) Our team of highly experienced individuals is dedicated to providing our clients with high-quality outsourced solutions today. To ensure that our clients receive the greatest services and grow their businesses, we employ the most modern technology and industry-best practices.
