What is an Employer of Record (EOR)?

An Employer of Record (EOR) is a service provider that acts as the official employer for a workforce in a particular country or region. By partnering with an EOR company like e-square, businesses can benefit from our expertise in local employment regulations, payroll administration, tax compliance and HR management. With our EOR services, you can focus on your core business while we handle the complexities of global employment.
